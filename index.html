TWO GARDENS, ONE PURPOSE FREE, FRESH FOOD FOR EVERYONE

Twelve Stones CDC operates two community gardens in Barbour County, Alabama (Eufaula and Clayton).
We also partner with relatedagencies to promote healthy eating habits and positive lifestyle choices.
We're a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization - your donations are tax deductible and much appreciated!

LEND A HAND DONATE OR VOLUNTEER

As a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, your donation is 100% tax deductible. Donations help us buy seeds, fertilizer, and supplies. The also help compensate local vendors who plow our fields and provide heavy maintenance.
Your volunteer time helps us to nurture and maintain the gardens until harvest time. Groups and Organizations can also 'Adopt a crop'!
