Since 2008

we have served the communities of Eufaula and Clayton, Alabama by providing fresh produce to all who care to help with the harvest.

Support the gardens!

No Rules Just Love

Thanks to our donors and volunteers, we’re able to grow and share corn, collards, peas, peppers, turnips and more. Every year we host a “Hoe-Down In The Garden” - everyone’s invited to share in the harvest.

TWO GARDENS, ONE PURPOSE FREE, FRESH FOOD FOR EVERYONE

Twelve Stones operates two community gardens in Barbour County, Alabama. We’re an certified nonprofit organization - your donations are tax deductible and much appreciated!
